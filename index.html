About WINDMI

WINDMI is a low-dimensional nonlinear model of the solar wind–magnetosphere–ionosphere system.

What the tool does

The WINDMI Web Tool reads OMNI solar-wind input over a selected UTC interval, computes the rectified solar-wind driving voltage vBs, solves the eight-state WINDMI ordinary differential equation system, and plots the selected output variables.

WINDMI equations

(1) \[ L\frac{dI}{dt}=vB_s(t)-V+M\frac{dI_1}{dt} \]
(2) \[ C\frac{dV}{dt}=I-I_1-I_{ps}-\Sigma V \]
(3) \[ \frac{3}{2}\frac{dp}{dt}=\frac{\Sigma V^2}{\Omega_{cps}}-u_0pK_{\parallel}^{1/2}\theta(I-I_c)-\frac{pVA_{eff}}{\Omega_{cps}B_{tr}L_y}-\frac{3p}{2\tau_E} \]
(4) \[ \frac{dK_{\parallel}}{dt}=I_{ps}V-\frac{K_{\parallel}}{\tau_{\parallel}} \]
(5) \[ L_1\frac{dI_1}{dt}=V-V_1+M\frac{dI}{dt} \]
(6) \[ C_1\frac{dV_1}{dt}=I_1-I_2-\Sigma_1V_1 \]
(7) \[ L_2\frac{dI_2}{dt}=V_1-(R_{prc}+R_{A2})I_2 \]
(8) \[ \frac{dW_{rc}}{dt}=R_{prc}I_2^2+\frac{pVA_{eff}}{B_{tr}L_y}-\frac{W_{rc}}{\tau_{rc}} \]

Input and trigger functions

(9) \[ vB_s=\begin{cases}V_0+L_y|v_x||B_z|, & B_z<0,\\ V_0, & B_z\ge 0.\end{cases} \]
(10) \[ \theta(I-I_c)=\frac{1}{2}\left[1+\tanh\left(\frac{I-I_c}{\Delta I}\right)\right] \]
